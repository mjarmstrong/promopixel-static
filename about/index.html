Our Story

From retail technology experts to revolutionizing promotional marketing

Founded by Best Friends with a Shared Vision

PromoPixel was born from the friendship and shared experiences of two high school best friends who found their paths converging in the intersection of retail and technology. After years of working in the retail industry and witnessing firsthand the challenges businesses face with promotional materials, they decided to combine their IT expertise to create something revolutionary.

Their experience in both the technical and retail sectors gave them unique insights into how artificial intelligence could transform the way businesses create and manage their promotional materials. This led to the birth of PromoPixel - a platform that makes professional-quality promotional design accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to democratize promotional design by combining cutting-edge AI technology with years of retail industry expertise. Our goal is to empower businesses to create stunning promotional materials quickly and efficiently, without compromising on quality or brand consistency.
